Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Remind Zen focuses on doing one thing really well — getting your reminders to your customers reliably and simply.

📱

Email + SMS reminders

Reach customers however they prefer — email, text message, or both at once. Each customer can have their own preference.

🗓

Automatic schedules

Set up recurring reminders once and let them run. Daily, weekly, monthly, or on a custom interval — your customers hear from you automatically.

👥

Any size customer list

Send to one customer or hundreds at once. Import your existing list from a spreadsheet in seconds and organize with tags and groups.

✏️

Reusable templates

Save your best messages as templates. Use variables like {name}, {date}, and {time} to personalize each reminder automatically.

📊

Delivery tracking

See every message sent, delivered, and failed in your send history. Know exactly what your customers received and when.

🔒

Opt-out management

Customers who want to stop receiving reminders are automatically respected. SMS consent and unsubscribe handling built in.

How it works

Up and running
in 3 simple steps.

No technical setup. No complicated integrations. Just a straightforward tool that works from day one.

1

Add your customers

Type them in one by one or import your existing spreadsheet. Takes about 2 minutes for most businesses.

2

Write your message

Use one of the built-in templates or write your own. Preview exactly what your customers will receive before sending.

3

Send or schedule

Send right now or set up an automatic schedule. Check delivery results in your history — everything is tracked.
